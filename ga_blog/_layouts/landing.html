The Mission

Although we wish we could solve everyone's problems, we recognize that is an impossible task. Instead, we aim to allieveate the streeses related to feeding our families. After all, no matter the size of your army, before you can go to battle, you first need breakfast.

It is with this in mind that we aim to support small businesses in our community and safely deliver nourishing meals to our children and neighbors who need them the most. We aim to provide our families with a moment of healing and hope that helps address kids' emotional needs as well as their physical ones.
